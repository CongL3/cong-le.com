A career sim for fans of the story, not the spreadsheet

Most basketball games hand you a whole franchise to babysit — trades, salary caps, and thirty rosters to micromanage. Hoop Quest goes the other way. You control one player and one story: the rise from a backyard prospect to an all-time legend. Every save is a fresh journey through school ball, campus seasons, the draft, and a long pro career, with a generated league world that keeps moving whether you sim through it or live every possession.

The choices are what make it stick. Pick your school program, decide how to spend each offseason, and make age-by-age calls that push your ratings, your bank balance, your popularity, and the trust you've built with the people around you. Fictional teams each have their own tempo, defense, prestige, and play style, so where you land shapes how your career unfolds. When you want the drama, drop into a live game and read the play-by-play; when you want to advance, sim a full week and jump to the next big moment.

It all runs in a compact, old-school package built for iPhone — chunky panels, arcade colours, stat rows, and scoreboards that load fast and get out of your way. There's no account to create and no roster license behind any of it: every team, player, and league is fictional, and your whole career lives on your device.

Frequently asked questions

What kind of basketball game is Hoop Quest?

A retro player-career RPG. You build a single player's career from backyard prospect to all-time legend — season progress, ratings, live games, roster context, and long-term legacy — without managing a real-world roster.

How do live games work?

Play possession-by-possession with box scores and play-by-play, or sim full weeks to move through long seasons quickly when you just want to advance your career.

Can I customize my player?

Yes. Create a custom player with a position, jersey number, and portrait style, then make age-by-age choices that affect ratings, money, popularity, and trust.

Are the teams and players real?

No. All teams, players, and leagues are fictional. No real-world league, team, or player license is used or implied — you choose fictional teams with different tempo, defense, prestige, and play styles.

Do I need an account or internet?

No account is required for core play, and your career data is stored on device.

What is the art style?

Compact, colorful, and old-school: chunky panels, arcade sports colors, stat rows, scoreboards, and a fast sim loop made for iPhone.
