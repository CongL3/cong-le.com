What Persona AI does

Persona AI: Story Chat is a private AI writing and story chat app for scenes, dialogue, prompts, and recurring creative threads on iPhone.

Use it to draft story ideas, explore fictional scenes, test dialogue, build writing prompts, and keep separate creative conversations organized. After model setup, chats run locally on your device with no account and no public feed.

Features

  • Start private AI story chats for scenes, dialogue, and writing prompts
  • Keep separate conversation history for different drafts and creative threads
  • Create reusable prompt profiles for tone, genre, and writing goals
  • Use Apple AI on supported devices
  • Import compatible local GGUF models
  • Use voice replies for hands-free listening
  • Export chats in Markdown or plain text

Free

  • Daily messages
  • Full chat history
  • Private local experience
  • Built-in model support on compatible devices

Pro

  • Unlimited messages
  • Local model library access
  • Custom prompt profiles
  • Chat export
  • Longer-form conversation tools

Privacy

  • No account required
  • No social feed
  • No tracking profile
  • Conversations stay on your device after setup

Notes

  • AI responses can be inaccurate and are not a substitute for professional advice.
  • Voice mode currently supports English input and output.
