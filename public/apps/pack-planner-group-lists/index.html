What Pack Planner does

Pack Planner helps families, friends, and travel groups pack together from one shared checklist.

Create a trip, invite other travelers with a link or code, assign items to people, and see who packed what before you leave. Private essentials can stay labelled for you, while shared items keep the whole group coordinated. Built-in templates cover weekend, business, beach, camping, international, family, and winter travel.

Free features:

  • Up to 3 active trips
  • Built-in packing templates
  • Invite-code group packing
  • Assignments, packed-by status, and private/shared labels
  • Local reminders before departure
  • Plain text sharing

Pack Planner Pro unlocks unlimited active trips, custom reusable templates, advanced reminders, and export preview.

Private trips stay on your device. Shared trips use Firebase Auth so your invite codes and group lists stay tied to your account.
