App-Specific Disclosure: Live Captions / Subtitles for Hearing Impaired

Live Captions is designed to caption speech on your device. The app does not include or call any third-party AI service for transcription, captioning, summarization, or content processing.

Data used for captioning

  • Microphone audio: Used only after you grant microphone permission, so the app can generate live captions. Microphone audio is processed on the device using Apple's on-device speech recognition frameworks.
  • Imported video audio: If you choose a video from Photos, the app extracts and transcribes the selected video's audio on the device to create captions.
  • Transcripts, captions, and saved sessions: Stored locally on your device when you use history or export features. You can delete saved sessions and recordings in the app.
  • Optional session recordings: If you enable audio recording, recordings are stored locally on your device for the retention period you choose. They are not uploaded by us.

No third-party AI sharing

We do not send microphone audio, imported video audio, generated transcripts, captions, saved sessions, optional recordings, or user content to OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, or any other third-party AI model or AI service.

Apple speech assets

The app uses Apple's on-device Speech framework. If a required Apple speech language asset is missing, iOS may download that model from Apple. This downloads a model to your device; it does not upload your audio, video, transcripts, or captions to Apple for processing.

Purchases

If you buy or restore Pro access, the app uses RevenueCat and the App Store to process purchase status, product identifiers, receipts, entitlement state, and related technical purchase metadata. RevenueCat does not receive your microphone audio, video audio, transcripts, captions, saved sessions, optional recordings, or AI prompt/content data.
