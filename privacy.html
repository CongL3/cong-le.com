This Privacy Policy explains how Cong Le ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our apps and related services.

This is a general policy for apps published under the Cong Le developer account. Specific apps may have app-specific disclosures in addition to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide

  • Account/profile information (if applicable).
  • App content you create (for example: reminders, notes, entries, settings).
  • Support messages you send to us.

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device type, operating system, app version, language, and region.
  • Usage events, crash diagnostics, and performance metrics.
  • Approximate technical metadata (IP-derived country/region).

1.3 Information from third parties

Some apps may use third-party SDKs (for analytics, crash reporting, ads, or subscription management) that process limited technical data under their own policies.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve app functionality.
  • Diagnose bugs, prevent abuse, and improve reliability.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

4. Data Retention and Security

  • We retain data only as long as needed for legitimate app operations, legal obligations, or dispute resolution.
  • We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data.
  • No method of storage/transmission is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your information, and to object to or restrict certain processing.

To request these actions, contact us at privacy@cong-le.com.

6. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction) unless explicitly stated in the app listing.

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date on this page.
